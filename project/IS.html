Information systems study the system to be implemented and how to integrate technology with this system
fully and efficiently.

The study of information systems includes the analysis and design of systems and the
operation of databases, networks, protection and scientific decisions.
This specialization is the link between the specialization of computer science and
the practical field of companies.

It is an understanding and study of the problems found in the business and the introduction of
technical solutions to this problem.
